Our Comprehensive
Interior Design Services

At AS Home Decor, we offer a complete range of interior design services to transform your spaces into beautiful, functional environments. Our team of experienced professionals works closely with you to understand your vision and bring it to life.

Architecture & Space Planning

Our architectural services focus on creating functional and aesthetically pleasing spaces that meet your specific needs. We specialize in:

  • Custom home design and renovation
  • Space optimization and layout planning
  • Structural modifications and improvements
  • Building code compliance and permits
  • 3D visualization and floor plans

Interior Design & Execution

Our interior design services transform spaces into beautiful, functional environments. We offer:

  • Complete interior design solutions
  • Custom furniture and cabinetry
  • Lighting design and installation
  • Material selection and sourcing
  • Project management and execution

Decor & Art Consultation

Our decor and art services add the perfect finishing touches to your space. We provide:

  • Custom artwork and installations
  • Accessory selection and placement
  • Color consultation and coordination
  • Textile and fabric selection
  • Seasonal decor updates

Why Choose AS Home Decor?

With years of experience in the industry, we bring expertise, creativity, and attention to detail to every project. Our team works closely with you to understand your vision and create spaces that reflect your style while meeting your functional needs.
