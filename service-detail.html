Crafted Spaces. Curated Aesthetics. Custom Details

At AS Home Decor, we transform spaces with purpose, personality, and precision. Whether you're looking to design a timeless home or a dynamic commercial space, our end-to-end interior design solutions are tailored to bring your vision to life.

We create layouts that go beyond looks—our designs are meticulously tailored to enhance workflow efficiency, optimize space usage, and improve the overall customer experience. Whether it’s a corporate office or a boutique space, our layout planning balances peak performance with visual appeal.

What Sets A.S. Home Decor Apart

  • Tailored Layout Designs.
  • Cohesive Aesthetic Integration.
  • Premium, Sustainable Materials.
  • Handcrafted Furniture & Woodwork.
  • Expert Restoration & Personalized Craftsmanship.

Long before 2018, I began a journey to merge creativity with craftsmanship. That passion led me to woodworking—and eventually, to the launch of A.S. Home Decor. From carving Sycamore and Mahogany to creating bespoke interiors, I bring years of experience, integrity, and attention to detail to every project.

Let’s Build Something Beautiful Whether you're designing a new space or restoring an old one, A.S. Home Decor is here to deliver custom solutions with soul.
