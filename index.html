Faq’s

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I get started with interior design services?

Getting started is simple! Contact us to schedule a consultation where we’ll discuss your vision, preferences, and requirements. From there, we’ll develop a concept, plan the space, and guide you through every step of the design process to bring your dream space to life

Q: Who leads A.S Home Decor?

Our firm was founded by Aman Ahmad Saifi, whose passion for detail and innovation continues to shape our approach to design.

Q: Can I choose my own furniture and decor items?

Absolutely! We encourage client collaboration and can integrate your preferred furniture and décor into the design. Additionally, we offer expert guidance on selecting pieces that complement your space for a cohesive and stylish look.

Q: What sets your interior design firm apart from others?

At A.S Home Decor, we blend creativity, functionality, and personalization to craft spaces that truly reflect your style. Our commitment to premium quality, client collaboration, and attention to detail ensures every project is unique, timeless, and tailored to perfection.
